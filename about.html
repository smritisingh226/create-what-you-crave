ABOUT US

Hi, I'm Himanshi! I am from New Delhi but I currently stay in Mumbai. I am the founder of CREATE what you crave.

I am a full time footwear designer and pursue my passion for photography and product styling through CREATE what you crave.

I started creating what I crave during 2020, when the pandemic hit us. Every business was moving online so I decided to help promote them through CREATE.

I help style products and click asthetically appealing images to help them promote their products.
